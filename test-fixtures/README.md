# test-fixtures/

Drop your own PDF/docx/xlsx files here for local, manual testing of the
engines. This directory's contents are gitignored (everything except this
README) — nothing you put here is ever tracked or committed.

Use your own documents, or any publicly-shareable sample you're comfortable
committing to disk locally. Do not use real third-party documents you don't
have the right to redistribute, even temporarily — this was a real problem
during development (actual third-party financial reports ended up
committed to this repo's history) and is exactly what this file exists to
prevent happening again.
